At Code&Serve, we don’t just build websites, we craft digital experiences. Founded in the heart of Antananarivo, Madagascar, our company was born from a simple belief: technology should empower, not complicate. That’s why we focus on creating web solutions that are not only visually stunning but also fast, functional, and user-focused. We specialize in web application development, UI/UX design, and custom software services for startups, SMEs, and visionary entrepreneurs. From landing pages that convert to powerful dashboards that drive decision-making, we bring ideas to life with clean code and creative energy. Our team is a dynamic mix of developers, designers, and thinkers, all committed to understanding your goals, anticipating your users’ needs, and delivering digital tools that make a difference. Whether you’re just starting out or scaling up, we’re here to partner with you at every stage of your journey.
